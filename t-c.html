Realm Rules

  1. Discord rules apply here as well, use common sense
  2. Be nice, fair and respectful to your fellow realm member’s and their builds.
  3. There will be absolutely no stealing, griefing, or duping of any kind under any circumstance.
  4. We love our server and want to keep it running great, so no building beyond the island.
  5. No Mega Builds or Farms unless authorized by a Moderator. #NoLagg
  6. If a creeper blows up fix the damage ASAP, and put a sign to message owner explaining what happened
  7. If any issues arise, tag or DM the moderators on discord immediately
